Hobbies

Throughout my life I've been in a lot of things, let's break down some of them.

First of all, I love videogames: I even did some esports and won a couple of regional Hearthstone tournaments.

I love music, listen to it pretty much as long as I am awake (right now too). My wife presented me a launchkey on my last birthday and I'm planning to use it to make some tunes on my own.

But the biggest, most important hobby of mine must be an intellectual games. There is a whole movement in CIS, where people compete in multiple formats. Some of them resemble of worldwide popular such as quizes or Jeopardy, but the most famous is definitely What? Where? When?

Inspired by the iconic everlasting russian show of the same name, this game assembles thousands of people to play and compete in pretty much every CIS city and even further: New York, London, Jerusalem and many more.
