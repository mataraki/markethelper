Biography

Hi, my name is Timur!

I was born on April 23, 1997 in a small town in Western Siberia. 5 years ago I moved to the Tyumen, capital of Western Siberia, to study IT. Since then I leave here.

It's not too bad down here as you may have heard: winter is only 6 months a year and we have enough steel to build the railroads in our spare time. We even have to eat sometimes. When the blizzards stops you can hunt some moose or bear to get yourself food for the whole week.

Just kidding. Tyumen is actually one of the most wealthy and comfortable cities to leave in Russia due to the state's amounts of oil and gas. Our universities have a lot of students and teachers from around the world too! City's motto is "Tyumen is best city on Earth", not sure about this one, but it's definitely fine.
